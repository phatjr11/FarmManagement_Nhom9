## How to use:
1. Open the **QR Scanner** form in the application.
2. Click **Start Scan** (Bắt đầu quét).
3. Point your webcam at one of the QR codes above.
4. If your webcam is not working, you can copy the **Raw Data** (e.g., `CROP-1`) and paste it into the **Manual Input** (Nhập mã thủ công) box.

> [!TIP]
> Make sure you have at least one crop and one animal record in your database with these IDs to see the full product details!
